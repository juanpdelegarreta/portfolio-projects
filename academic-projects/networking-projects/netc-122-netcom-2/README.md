# NETC-122 Network Communications 2
## Course Description
A continuation of NETC 121. Topics include: access control lists, routing protocols, spanning tree, VLANs and network security, and network address translation.

## Assignments Overview
Labs are instructional walkthroughs of setting up networks based on weekly topic. Lab reports contain screenshots and answers to lab questions. Lab files and Lab sims are Packet Tracer assignments.

## Weekly Syllabus
- Week 1 – ACL’s  
- Week 2 – NAT/PAT
- Week 3 – Point to Point WAN’s / Serial Communication
- Week 4 – EtherChannel, RSTP, DHCP
- *Week 5 – Test 1*
- Week 6 – VTP / Router and switch file systems
- Week 7 – Advanced OSPF
- Week 8 – Advanced EIGRP
- Week 9 – Advanced BGP
- *Week 10 – Test 2*
- Week 11 – Tiered Networking
- Week 12 – Logging and Network Monitoring
- Week 13 – Wireless Networks
- Week 14 – IP Version 6
- *Week 15 – Test 3*
