# NETC-180
## Course Description
A course on methods for analyzing and classifying organizational data to maintain information security. Topics include: information ownership; information threats, vulnerabilities, and exposure; and investigating and assessing risk.

## Assignments Overview
Module assignments include research, documentation, and applying concepts to create risk management and cybersecurity documents.

## Modulely Syllabus
- 	Module 1 – What is Risk?
- 	Module 2 – Qualitative vs Quantitative Risk and Risk Matrices
- 	Module 3 – Data Classifications
- 	Module 4 – Security Controls
- 	Module 5 – Risk Frameworks
- 	Module 6 – Risk Assessments and Risk Registers
- 	Module 7 – Metrics and KPIs
- 	Module 8 – Residual Risk and Efficacy of Controls
-	Module 9 – Third-Party Risk Management
- 	Module 10 – Corporate Risk Management Plan
